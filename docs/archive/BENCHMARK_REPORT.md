## Methodology

### Benchmark Compositions

Each vertical is represented by a carefully designed composition that exercises representative features:

- **aerospace**: Satellite digital twins, zero-G simulation - scientific visualization and IoT
- **architecture**: BIM visualization and architectural walkthroughs - high-fidelity rendering platforms
- **art**: Virtual galleries and 3D art visualization - web and high-quality rendering platforms
- **automotive**: Vehicle configurators and AR showrooms - consumer platforms with high-quality rendering
- **education**: Interactive learning experiences - broad platform support including web for accessibility
- **entertainment**: Concerts, events, social VR experiences - immersive entertainment platforms
- **fitness**: VR fitness training with body/hand tracking - XR fitness platforms
- **gaming**: Full-featured game engines and social VR platforms
- **healthcare**: AR/VR medical training, patient monitoring, surgical planning - mobile and headset platforms
- **manufacturing**: Digital twins, robotics simulation, IoT integration - industrial platforms
- **real-estate**: Virtual property tours and AR staging - consumer AR and web platforms
- **retail**: AR product preview and virtual try-on - consumer mobile and AR platforms
- **social**: Social VR spaces and multiplayer experiences - networked VR platforms
- **tourism**: AR historical sites and virtual tours - consumer mobile and web platforms

### Metrics

- **Compilation Time**: Measured using `performance.now()` from parse to output generation
- **Output Size**: Total byte count of generated code/data
- **Feature Parity**: Percentage of HoloScript features (traits, environment settings, etc.) present in compiled output

### Platform Applicability

Not all platforms are suitable for all verticals. The target mapping is based on:

- Industry standard platform choices for each vertical
- Platform capabilities (AR, VR, mobile, desktop, robotics)
- Real-world deployment scenarios
